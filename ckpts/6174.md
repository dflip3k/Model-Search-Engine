I saw different mixes built on Anything v4 or v4.5 and i've realized what i truly need. But the main differences between Any | AOM and DivineElegance was the backgrounds.</p><p>DivineElegance at the beginning was a mix for my personal use, but it was warmly received by Unstable Diffusion's discord community and some time later i decided to share it, due to many requests.</p><p>Right now DivineElegance is my favourite model and quite unique thing for me.
